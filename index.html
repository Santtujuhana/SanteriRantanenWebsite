About Me

Engineering the future,
from metal to quantum.

I'm a Finnish mechanical engineer with a versatile career spanning heavy industrial machinery, precision medical devices, and cutting-edge quantum technology. My journey has taken me through some of Finland's most respected engineering companies — from Kone's elevators to Bluefors' dilution refrigerators.

Today, I work at Bluefors on the measurement infrastructure team, helping build the physical framework that enables quantum computing research. My days involve designing components that operate near absolute zero — where classical physics gives way to quantum mechanics.

Outside engineering, I'm passionate about robotics and photography — two fields that combine technical precision with creative expression.
